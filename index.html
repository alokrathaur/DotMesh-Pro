How you play

Three ways
to compete

01 Local Multiplayer

2–4 players,
one device

Pass and play on a single phone or tablet. Up to four players take turns drawing lines — claim a box on all four sides and it's yours. First to capture the most boxes wins. Fast, tactical, and no internet required.

Each player sets their name, picks an emoji avatar, and chooses from 12 color options (6 free, 6 Pro). The scoreboard updates live as boxes are captured, with a pulse indicator showing whose turn it is. Ties are handled gracefully with a dedicated result screen.

2–4 players Custom names & emojis 12 color options Live scoreboard Tie resolution No internet needed
02 Bot Play

Challenge
the machine

Three difficulty levels — Easy, Medium, and Hard. The AI reads the board and responds accordingly: Easy lets you practice openings, Medium forces deliberate play, and Hard applies real strategic pressure with chain control.

Perfect for solo sessions, learning the game, or warming up before online play. The bot takes its turn instantly — no waiting, no drop-off risk. Jump in and out whenever.

Easy / Medium / Hard Adaptive AI strategy Instant turns Solo anytime No account needed
03 Online Multiplayer

Play live,
anywhere

Create a room and share a 6-character invite code. Your opponent joins on their device — anywhere in the world. Moves sync instantly via Firebase Realtime: you see their line appear the moment they draw it.

Both players see the same live board with their respective color highlights. The game starts the moment both players are in the room. No lobbies, no bots filling empty slots — just two people, competing.

Real-time sync Room code invite Share via any app Global matchmaking Firebase powered Low latency

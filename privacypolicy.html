Overview

DotMesh: Dots & Boxes Game ("DotMesh", "we", "our", or "us") is developed by LegendPrix AI. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

By using DotMesh, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to operate the game. Specifically:

  • Game data — scores, match results, and preferences stored locally on your device.
  • Online multiplayer — temporary room codes and real-time game state transmitted to our servers only for the duration of an active match. We do not require an account or login.
  • Purchase information — processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We receive only a confirmation of entitlement status via RevenueCat. We never see your payment details.
  • Crash & diagnostic data — anonymised crash reports to improve stability (via Expo/EAS).
  • Advertising data — if you are on the free tier, ads are served by Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. DotMesh Pro users see no ads.

How We Use Your Information

  • To enable real-time online multiplayer gameplay
  • To verify your DotMesh Pro subscription status
  • To fix bugs and improve game performance
  • To serve relevant ads to free-tier players (via AdMob)

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

Third-Party Services

DotMesh integrates the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — subscription management. Privacy Policy →
  • Google AdMob — ad delivery for free users. Privacy Policy →
  • Firebase — real-time online multiplayer infrastructure. Privacy Policy →
  • Expo / EAS — crash reporting and app delivery. Privacy Policy →

Data Retention & Storage

Game preferences and settings are stored locally on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app. Temporary multiplayer session data is deleted from our servers immediately after a match ends. We do not maintain any user accounts or persistent user profiles.

Children's Privacy

DotMesh is a casual game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require registration, and no personal data is stored on our servers. Ad personalisation for younger users is handled in accordance with applicable platform policies (COPPA, GDPR-K).

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete data associated with you. Since DotMesh does not create user accounts, most data is stored on your own device. For any privacy-related requests, contact us at:
